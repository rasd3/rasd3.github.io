Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Signal Processing and Artificial Intelligence Lab, advised by Junwon Choi, at Hanyang University. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Hanyang University.

My research interests lie in 3D object detection in autonomous driving environments, and I have conducted studies across sensor-fusion, temporal, and domain adaptation. Additionally, I completed an internship with the autonomous driving development team at Kakao Mobility.
